About the Repository

Purpose, authorship and publication principles for the Lal Lal Wind Farm Record.

The publisher

This repository is published by LEWIS TROJAN HORSE PTY LTD as Trustee of the Owen Robert Inter Vivos Trust.

The repository has been established to preserve and publish the continuing administrative and evidentiary record concerning the Lal Lal Wind Farm in an organised, accessible and version-controlled form.

What this repository is not

It is not the official website of the Lal Lal Wind Farm, the operator, any regulator, the Victorian Ombudsman, VAGO, DTP, EPA, Council or any public authority.

It is not intended to substitute for statutory decision-making, regulatory enforcement, legal proceedings or independent technical assessment.

Publication principles

Documentary integrity

The site is structured so that a reader can distinguish historical source material, Trustee-authored documents, FOI decision records and publicly available third-party material.

H

Historical source record

Beneficiary actions and historical FOIs are recorded as source material predating Trustee consolidation.

T

Trustee record

Trustee-authored statements, memoranda, annexes, minutes and notices are recorded as the organised administrative record.

P

Public repository

The website provides a stable public index and publication platform for the continuing record.

Publication statement

Documents and descriptions published here are presented for administrative record purposes. Later supplementary material is added to the continuing record and does not retrospectively alter earlier executed material unless expressly recorded by a subsequent Trustee Minute or document control note.

Where third-party documents are referenced, the repository will generally identify source, provenance, request pathway and integration into the Trustee record. Underlying released FOI documents are not automatically republished.

Published by LEWIS TROJAN HORSE PTY LTD as Trustee of the Owen Robert Inter Vivos Trust.
