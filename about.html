About the Repository

This website is maintained as an independent administrative publication repository by LEWIS TROJAN HORSE PTY LTD as Trustee.

Repository purpose

The repository publishes the Trustee's continuing administrative and evidentiary record concerning the Lal Lal Wind Farm. It is intended to preserve documentary chronology, version control and public accessibility of completed administrative material.

The repository is not operated by Lal Lal Wind Farm Pty Ltd, the wind farm operator, any regulator or any government agency.

Publication methodology

Documents are organised according to source, version, date and administrative purpose. Later publications supplement rather than silently replace earlier material unless expressly recorded.

Documents authored by the Trustee are distinguished from public source material and third-party documents.

Published by LEWIS TROJAN HORSE PTY LTD as Trustee of the Owen Robert Inter Vivos Trust.

This repository is an independent administrative publication. It is not the official website of the Lal Lal Wind Farm, the operator, a regulator or a government agency.
