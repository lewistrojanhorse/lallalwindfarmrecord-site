About the Record

Information about the purpose, publisher and publication methodology of this repository.

Purpose

The Lal Lal Wind Farm Record has been established as an independent repository of administrative and evidentiary publications relating to the Lal Lal Wind Farm.

The repository is intended to preserve published administrative records, source references and associated documentation in an organised and accessible form.

Publisher

LEWIS TROJAN HORSE PTY LTD
(as Trustee)

This site is independent and is not operated by the wind farm, a regulator or a government agency.
