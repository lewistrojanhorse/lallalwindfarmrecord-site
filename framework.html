Understanding the record

Regulatory Framework

Plain English guide to the governing planning and technical framework for the Lal Lal Wind Farm record.

How the framework fits together

Law, permit, technical standard, endorsed plan, assessment and audit

The repository separates the governing framework from later documentary observations. This page explains the pathway before the reader turns to the evidence.

1

Planning law

The planning permit establishes the project-specific legal framework and incorporates operational noise conditions.

2

EPA regulations

The Environment Protection Regulations framework records statutory duties for wind turbine noise, including assessment, management, reporting and auditor functions.

3

Technical methodology

NZS 6808:2010 and the endorsed NCTP supply the technical language and compliance assessment pathway.

EPA wind turbine noise regulations

Statutory source document added to the framework

The Environment Protection Amendment (Wind Turbine Noise) Regulations 2022 are published in the repository as a core regulatory source document. The Regulations amended the Environment Protection Regulations 2021 to specify matters relating to wind turbine noise from wind energy facilities, including the relevant noise standard, noise limits, post-construction noise assessment, noise management plans, annual statements, periodic monitoring and environmental auditor functions.

Assessment

The Regulations record duties concerning post-construction noise assessment and compliance assessment by suitably qualified and experienced acousticians.

Management

The Regulations record the noise management plan framework, including procedures for complaints, risk identification, compliance assessment and noise reduction where non-compliance is detected.

Audit and reporting

The Regulations record annual statement requirements, periodic monitoring obligations and environmental auditor functions relevant to post-construction assessment, noise management plans and monitoring.

Why this matters

Every issue is read against the same pathway

K15aa, SAC assessment, NMP custody, environmental auditor verification, permit amendment and FOI disclosure are each read by tracing the same path from the governing requirement to the disclosed documentary record.
