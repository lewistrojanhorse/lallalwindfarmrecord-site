Understanding the record

Regulatory Framework

Plain English guide to the governing planning and technical framework for the Lal Lal Wind Farm record.

How the framework fits together

Law, permit, technical standard, endorsed plan, assessment and audit

The repository separates the governing framework from later documentary observations. This page explains the pathway before the reader turns to the evidence.

1

Planning law

The planning permit establishes the legal framework for the wind farm and incorporates operational noise conditions.

2

Technical standard

NZS 6808:2010 supplies the governing technical language and assessment framework adopted by the permit.

3

Endorsed methodology

The Noise Compliance Testing Plan sets out the compliance assessment methodology endorsed under the planning framework.

Why this matters

Every issue is read against the same pathway

K15aa, SAC assessment, NMP custody, environmental auditor verification, permit amendment and FOI disclosure are each read by tracing the same path from the governing requirement to the disclosed documentary record.
