Core pathways

The main issues in the disclosed record

These issues are grouped so a reader can see how the planning framework, technical standard, testing plan, assessment record, auditor material and FOI record connect.

K

K15aa background sound

The K15aa page is the benchmark evidentiary page. It traces the NCTP requirement, replacement monitoring, later reliance and disclosure record.
Z

NZS 6808:2010 definitions

Explains the technical language used across the record, including background sound, receiver locations and the assessment framework.

N

NCTP mandatory requirements

The Noise Compliance Testing Plan is the endorsed methodology connecting the permit to later compliance assessment.

S

Special Audible Characteristics

The SAC pathway concerns attended observations, monitoring periods, reporting and whether the disclosed record enables independent verification.
P

Permit amendment

The amendment record matters because it concerns the proposed removal of operational noise conditions while documentary questions remain recorded.
F

FOI and custody record

The FOI pathway shows what agencies held, disclosed, relied upon, or reported as not located within the administrative record.

Why this structure matters

The repository does not substitute assertion for the source record. Each issue is presented so a reader can move from plain-English explanation to the governing framework, then to evidence pages and source documents.

Where the disclosed record is incomplete, the site records the documentary position rather than filling gaps with assumption.
