Chronology

Administrative Chronology

A structured chronology distinguishing historical source material, Trustee actions and public repository publication.

Reading note

Chronology by record stream

This chronology is an index-level public summary. It is intended to guide readers to the underlying executed record and should not be treated as replacing the Supporting Record, Annexes or documentary verification material.

Part I — Historical administrative recordPart II — Trustee administrative recordPart III — Public repository
2018

Endorsed NCTP framework

The Noise Compliance Test Plan forms part of the operative post-construction noise compliance framework and is repeatedly referenced in the record as the approved methodology.

August 2019

PCNA monitoring program triggered

The Supporting Record records CRG material describing the PCNA monitoring program as triggered by commissioning of the last turbine.

October 2019

Construction complete / commissioning continuing

CRG material is recorded as describing construction as complete while commissioning remained ongoing.

2019 monitoring periods

K15aa and turbine operation context

Stage 2 Appendix H records aspects of early commissioning, a 5 MW exemption and operation of up to 15 turbines during 2019 monitoring periods.

October–December 2020

Interim monitoring during constrained operation

SLR interim monitoring is recorded during constrained operation under AEMO ramp-up conditions.

10 December 2020

Commissioning completion statement

LLWF later stated, in response to EPA IGN-00008382, that commissioning was completed on 10 December 2020.

18 October 2022

Noise Management Plan dated

The disclosed Noise Management Plan is dated 18 October 2022 and forms a central part of the EPA reliance and NMP custody chronology.

22 and 28 June 2022

Elaine auditor verification statements

The record identifies Stage 1 Elaine verification statements that expressly refer to regulation 131D(2).

March 2023–January 2024

Stage 2 monitoring intervals

Annex B Version 1.1 records Stage 2 monitoring campaigns and additional SAC observations during this period.

13 November 2023

Elaine revised verification

A revised Stage 1 Elaine verification is recorded following correction of a K15aa transcription issue.

7 December 2023

Yendon verification

Stage 1 Yendon verification is recorded, including further measurement context.

31 January 2024

EPA reliance on NMP

EPA correspondence identified the NMP as a document relied upon in assessing compliance before later confirmation of completed auditor verification.

8 July 2024

DTP NCTP secondary consent correspondence

DTP correspondence recorded proposed NCTP changes under consideration as part of a secondary consent amendment.

20 and 24 September 2024

Stage 2 assessment and auditor verification

Stage 2 post-construction assessment and auditor verification material form part of the combined facility record.

25 September 2025

EPA FOI-100-2025 decision

EPA identified nine documents in response to FOI-100-2025; later OVIC material records document identification outcomes.

2 October 2025

Permit amendment application

The amendment application sought amendment of Condition 23, deletion of Conditions 24–27 and insertion of Condition 27A.

21 January 2026

Trustee regulatory record placed

The Trustee issued a formal Statement of Harm and related administrative notices forming part of the 2026 Trustee record.

19 February 2026

Statement of Matters of Public Interest

The Trustee prepared the Statement of Matters of Public Interest as the primary public-interest record.

7 March 2026

SRAM Version 1.0

Initial Supporting Record and Analytical Memorandum executed.

16 March 2026

SRAM Version 1.1

Supplementary contextual addendum executed.

17 March 2026

Agency communications

Trustee correspondence and annex material were communicated to relevant agencies as part of the continuing administrative record.

28 April 2026

OVIC determination — EPA FOI-100-2025

OVIC dismissed the complaint under section 61B(1)(b) and recorded search and document identification outcomes.

18 May 2026

DTP administrative clarification request

Trustee correspondence sought clarification concerning the permit amendment transition and related document record.

6 June 2026

SRAM Version 1.2

Supplementary version recorded FOI, OVIC, custody and regulatory transition reconciliation.

25 June 2026

EPA FOI-010-2026 final release

EPA advised third-party review rights had expired and remaining documents could be released.

26 June 2026

DTP clarification record closure

The Trustee recorded no acknowledgement or substantive response to the administrative clarification correspondence as at that date.

29 June 2026

SRAM Version 1.3 and record crystallisation

The Trustee’s current administrative and evidentiary record was integrated and treated as crystallised as at 29 June 2026.

3 July 2026

DTP FOI CS019241 decision

DTP granted access in part, located 27 documents and recorded that the revised NCTP was formally withdrawn in July 2024 and never endorsed.

4 July 2026

Public repository established

The public domain and repository were established to publish the continuing administrative and evidentiary record.

Status note: the updated Ombudsman package is not recorded as issued in this chronology until the Trustee has in fact issued it. Future agency communications should be added as dated supplementary entries.
