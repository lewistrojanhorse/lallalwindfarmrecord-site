Record structure

Why documents are grouped

Documents are organised according to their role within the documentary pathway rather than simply by date. This allows readers to move from the governing framework through technical assessment, regulatory material and Freedom of Information disclosures in a logical sequence.

Record principle

The website explains the record. The PDFs are the record.

Each document below is provided for direct public access. The administrative and evidentiary record was crystallised as at 29 June 2026 and transmitted to the Victorian Ombudsman on 6 July 2026 as a Supplementary Public Interest Submission.

Reading pathway: New readers should begin with Start Here, use the Glossary for technical terms, read Understanding the Record for methodology, and then return to this document library for the source PDFs.

Record principle

Documentary integrity

Original documents are published in their original form. The record explains their context but does not alter, annotate or highlight the underlying documents.
