FOI Register

Freedom of Information Register

A provenance register for FOI requests, agency decisions and integration into the Trustee record.

Publication approach

Decision notices and provenance, not indiscriminate document republication

The repository records FOI requests, decision notices, outcomes and incorporation into the administrative record. Underlying released documents are not automatically republished unless separately assessed as appropriate.

Privacy and publication note: FOI decision notices may be published where appropriate, with personal information or sensitive details redacted where necessary. The primary purpose is to record documentary provenance.

Historical FOIs

Historical FOIs undertaken by the Beneficiary or prior to full Trustee consolidation are retained as part of the historical administrative record. They are important because they explain how the evidentiary record came to be assembled.

Trustee FOIs

Trustee FOIs are recorded as part of the continuing Trustee administrative and evidentiary record. They are indexed by agency, reference, decision outcome and the annex or memorandum into which the material was incorporated.
