Independent Documentary Verification

The Documentary Verification Schedule asks whether an independent reader can verify the mandatory requirements from the disclosed administrative record.

Purpose

What this page does

The Verification Schedule is not a compliance finding. It is a structured method for comparing mandatory planning and technical requirements with the disclosed documentary record.

Fact

The Schedule reviews mandatory requirements drawn from the planning permit, NZS 6808:2010 and the NCTP.

Why it matters

It gives readers a repeatable way to ask whether each requirement can be independently verified from the disclosed record.

Evidence

The Schedule is supported by the Statement, SRAMs, Annexes, FOI material and primary technical documents.

Terminology correction

The repository uses the NZS terminology noise-sensitive location. It does not use the incorrect phrase “noise sensitive assessment”.
