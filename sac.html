Special Audible Characteristics

SAC Record and Mandatory Observation Procedure

How Special Audible Characteristics are treated within NZS 6808:2010, the approved NCTP and the Trustee's Annex B record.

Plain English

What SAC means

Special Audible Characteristics are identifiable features of wind farm noise, such as tonality or modulation, that require specific attention under the compliance framework.

Fact

The approved NCTP contains procedures for SAC identification and reporting.

Why it matters

The NCTP procedure includes mandatory attended listening observations. The question for the repository is what the disclosed record shows about those observations.

Evidence

Annex B and Annex B Version 1.1 organise the SAC record, including attended-observation material, timing, reporting and evidentiary limits.

Mandatory attended listening observation issue

The SAC page should be read through the NCTP procedure: mandatory requirement, documentary record, further reading.
