Noise Compliance Testing Plan

The NCTP is the bridge between the planning permit, NZS 6808 and the operational compliance record.

Endorsed methodology

The NCTP is not just another report

The Noise Compliance Testing Plan is the endorsed operational noise testing methodology incorporated into the planning framework. It records how operational noise monitoring, analysis and reporting were to be undertaken.

24

Prepared before development

The planning framework required a Noise Compliance Testing Plan before development commenced.

25

Auditor review and approval

The NCTP framework included review by an EPA-accredited auditor and approval by the Minister for Planning.

R

Reporting pathway

The NCTP prescribed operational assessment and reporting obligations following commissioning.

Why this page matters

The NCTP is the bridge between the permit and the evidence

The planning permit supplies the legal obligation, NZS 6808 supplies the technical language, and the NCTP supplies the operational methodology. That is why the Trustee's record repeatedly returns to the NCTP when examining K15aa, SAC, reporting and auditor verification.
