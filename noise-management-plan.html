Noise Management Plan

A source-led guide to the available October 2022 Noise Management Plan, its relationship with the endorsed Noise Compliance Testing Plan, EPA documentary custody, auditor verification correspondence, Freedom of Information releases and the later planning permit amendment pathway.

Reading position: This page distinguishes four separate matters: the governing framework, the technical content of the NMP, documentary custody and later administrative reliance. It does not determine compliance, validity, breach or legal effect.
Document identity

The available document

The document released through the EPA Freedom of Information record is titled Lal Lal Wind Farm — Noise Management Plan, Project No. 0467385, Version 3.0, dated 18 October 2022. Its document history records Draft, Final 01, Final 02 and Final 03 revisions.

What the NMP says it does

The NMP states that it was prepared under regulation 131E(5) of the Environment Protection Regulations 2021 and combines a noise compliance test plan component with a complaints management component.

It also states that the endorsed Noise Compliance Testing Plan remains the complete operational measurement procedure and that, where a conflict exists, the NCTP takes precedence.

Background-noise documentary pathway

Documents identified by the NMP

The October 2022 NMP identifies the Marshall Day Acoustics Background Noise Monitoring Report and the endorsed Noise Compliance Testing Plan as the principal background-noise and operational-limit references. The SLR K15aa Baseline Report dated 16 February 2021 is not identified in the NMP reference list or its stated background-noise documentary framework.

EPA documentary custody

NMP chronology

The dates matter because later EPA descriptions of the NMP post-date the October 2022 document and the earlier EPA reliance record.

18 October 2022

Version 3.0 prepared

The available NMP is dated 18 October 2022 and records Final Revision 03.

November 2023

Verification requested

EPA later stated, in correspondence dated 21 June 2024, that it asked for auditor verification of the NMP in November 2023.

31 January 2024

EPA reliance record

EPA identified the NMP, by project number 0467385, among documents it had used when assessing compliance.

22 May 2024

Revisions said to be continuing

EPA stated that the NMP was still undergoing revisions and had not yet been reviewed by an EPA-appointed environmental auditor.

21 June 2024

Earlier verification request confirmed

EPA stated that auditor verification had been requested in November 2023.

23 January 2025

Internal review description

EPA's internal review correspondence stated that EPA had seen a draft NMP but had not been provided with a completed final NMP.

4 February 2025

Date of draft clarified

EPA advised that the draft NMP was dated October 2022.

FOI-002-2024 and FOI-100-2025

Same October 2022 document disclosed

The available releases identified by the repository contain the same Version 3.0 NMP dated 18 October 2022.

Transitional Condition 27A

The later permit amendment pathway refers to continuing compliance with the endorsed NCTP and complaints plan until an NMP has been prepared and implemented in accordance with regulation 131E.
