The question at the centre of this record

Do the available documents show that the Lal Lal Wind Farm's planning, technical and regulatory obligations are being met?

This website was created to help answer that question.

It shows the requirements, the evidence found, the gaps identified, the questions put to regulators and other organisations, and the responses received — or where no response was received.

Why this website was created

To show the gaps in the record

What the website does

The Lal Lal Wind Farm is governed by planning approvals, technical standards, noise requirements and regulatory duties.

The Trustee examined the available documents to see whether they explain how those obligations are being met.

Where the documents do not provide a clear answer, the website identifies the gap.

What happened when questions were asked

The website also shows:
the requests made to regulators and other organisations, the answers received, and where a question was not answered.

This allows readers to see the gap, the question asked about it, and the response — or lack of response — in the same record.

Issued 16 July 2026

Notice — Documentary Reconciliation of EPA's Current Regulatory Position

Lal Lal Wind Farm — K15aa

This Notice asks EPA Victoria to identify the documents that explain how its current position is reconciled with the laws, planning requirements, NZS 6808:2010 and other documents applying to K15aa.

It records a request for a documentary explanation. It does not make a compliance finding.

How the website is written

Plain English, backed by the documents

The website uses plain English wherever possible. Exact legal or technical wording is used only when it needs to be preserved.

The documents are published so readers can check the evidence, see the gaps and read the responses for themselves.
