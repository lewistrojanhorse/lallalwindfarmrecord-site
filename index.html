Repository centrepiece

Independent Documentary Verification

The repository is organised around one central question: can an independent reader verify the mandatory planning and technical requirements from the disclosed administrative and documentary record?

The verification pathway

The Documentary Verification Schedule connects the governing law, Planning Permit, NZS 6808:2010, the endorsed Noise Compliance Testing Plan, post-construction assessment, environmental auditor material, Freedom of Information records and the Trustee's administrative record.

Important: The Schedule does not determine compliance, breach, liability, legal rights or regulatory findings. It records whether the disclosed record enables independent reconciliation of mandatory requirements.

Repository principle

Evidence before opinion

This site is designed to make the administrative record easy to follow. Statements are presented as documentary facts, statutory or technical framework references, or administrative observations. The repository does not substitute its own findings for the disclosed record.
